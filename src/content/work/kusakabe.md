Kusakabe is a Japanese restaurant in San Francisco. It is known for being the first restaurant in San Francisco to exclusively serve an Omakase.

Built a custom WordPress site to manage the restaurant's menu, reservations, and info pages. The site was designed to be user-friendly and visually appealing, with a focus on the restaurant's unique dining experience.
